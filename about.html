About

Clayton Hebenik

Fresh out of graduating from the University of Lethbridge with a BFA in New Media in 2011, I began working in Calgary, Alberta learning the ropes of web design and front-end in a super lean agency.

After stumbling through the “joys” of freelancing and independent contracting for a couple of years, I opted to pursue further education through the Master of Digital Media program through the Centre of Digital Media in 2017 focusing my studies on Agile collaboration and User Experience Design.

Most recently I worked as a Digital Designer on Best Buy Canada’s Retail E-Commerce experiences, lending my expertise in visual design and Agile processes to crafting landing pages and online promotional graphics/assets to aid Canadians in the journey discovering and purchasing the tech that enriches their lives.

Residing in Vancouver, British Columbia I’ve always had a passion and interest in the outdoors and being active. When I’m not working you can usually find me exploring both the city and mountains, either on skateboard, bicycle, or foot; often with a camera (or three) in tow.
