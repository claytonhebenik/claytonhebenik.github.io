Podcast Network

2019

With the launch of a podcast network and Patreon campaign, the Hard Times needed a way to showcase and promote new episodes on site.

To solve this a visuall distinct component was added to the sidebar to list new episodes from the various podcasts. A landing page was also created to learn more about the Podcast Network and Patreon and give readers an option to subscribe or listen to the latest episodes.
